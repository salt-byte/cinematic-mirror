## App 描述（中文）

影中镜，是一面映照你灵魂穿搭的镜子。

在这里，一位年轻的AI造型导演"陆野"将通过一场沉浸式"试镜"对话，深入了解你的性格、审美品味与生活态度，为你生成一份专属的"影中人格档案"——找到最契合你的电影角色与造型方案。

✦ 沉浸式AI试镜
与造型导演陆野进行8-12轮深度对话，从审美品质、行为风格、过往经历、面对挑战到人生态度，全方位探索你的穿搭人格。

✦ 影中人格档案
AI为你生成一份诗意而精准的人格分析报告，包含核心叙事、五维度解读、视觉建议，以及最契合你的电影角色匹配。

✦ 电影角色匹配
从精心策划的电影角色数据库中，找到与你气质最吻合的角色，附带详细的造型方案——色彩、材质、剪裁、导演点评。

✦ 专业穿搭咨询
基于你的档案，随时向陆野咨询日常穿搭建议。他会用电影美学的视角，给出具体到颜色、材质、款式的专业方案。

✦ 视频穿搭咨询
开启摄像头，与AI造型师面对面交流，获得即时的穿搭指导与分析。

影中镜，让你的每一天都像一帧精心设计的电影画面。

## App Description (English)

Cinematic Mirror — a mirror that reflects your soul's style.

Here, a young AI styling director named "Lu Ye" conducts an immersive "audition" conversation to deeply understand your personality, aesthetic taste, and life philosophy, generating an exclusive "Cinematic Personality Profile" — finding the movie character and styling that best fits you.

✦ Immersive AI Audition
Engage in 8-12 rounds of deep conversation with director Lu Ye, exploring your style personality across aesthetics, behavior, experiences, resilience, and life philosophy.

✦ Cinematic Personality Profile
AI generates a poetic yet precise personality analysis report, including core narrative, five-dimensional insights, visual recommendations, and your best-matching movie character.

✦ Movie Character Matching
From a curated database of iconic movie characters, discover the one whose essence aligns with yours — complete with detailed styling proposals covering color palettes, materials, tailoring, and director's notes.

✦ Professional Styling Consultation
Based on your profile, consult Lu Ye anytime for daily outfit advice. Using cinematic aesthetics, he provides specific recommendations on colors, fabrics, and silhouettes.

✦ Video Styling Consultation
Turn on your camera for face-to-face conversations with your AI stylist, receiving instant styling guidance and analysis.

Cinematic Mirror — make every day of your life look like a carefully designed film frame.
